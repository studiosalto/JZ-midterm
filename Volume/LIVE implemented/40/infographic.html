Architecture of Peace Reloaded

Justice


Four strategies: [1] Plan across the devide, [2] Share space, [3] Share history and [4] Create a third space
The question of who owns a certain space, who may lay claim to a specific territory and who rules it is pivotal to ethno-national conflicts.

This singular link between territory and identity engenders contradictions, especially in urban contexts, since cities, in the course of their turbulent histories, have repeatedly integrated newly arrived and diverse population groups and so different needs are layered.
